How earnings settle

Each completed job credits your balance in halala-precision. Failed jobs and rejected requests don’t bill the renter, so they don’t credit you either. Balance updates within a few seconds of job completion. Anything you see on this page is real and ready to be paid out.

Revenue share

At your current Silver tier, you keep 75% of every job. The rest covers platform routing, monitoring, billing, and renter support. The cut shown on each job already nets these out — what you see is what you’ll be paid.
